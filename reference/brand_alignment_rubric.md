# Brand Alignment & Aesthetic Rubric

## Scoring Instructions
For every inbound pitch, assign 1 point per pillar if the concept is clearly present. Assign 0 if absent, vague, or contradicted. Do not award partial points. The operator must record the exact score in the output block.

## 5-Point Brand Alignment Pillars

| # | Pillar | Presence Indicator (Score 1) | Contradiction (Score 0) |
|---|--------|------------------------------|-------------------------|
| 1 | Christian Faith Integration | Mentions Jesus, scripture, prayer, church, gospel, faith-based wellness, or Christian community | Mentions New Age, astrology, manifesting, "the universe," secular-only with no room for faith |
| 2 | Intentional Living / Slow Living | Mentions intentionality, slowing down, digital detox, Sabbath, simplicity, anti-hustle | Mentions growth hacking, scaling fast, "crushing it," aggressive productivity |

| 5 | Minimalist Design / Human Storytelling | Mentions clean design, minimalism, white space, typography, personal testimony, quiet aesthetic | Mentions loud vectors, neon colors, cluttered layouts, stock-photo-heavy, corporate clinical tone |

## Text-Based Aesthetic Surrogates

Because inbound pitches are text-only, the operator cannot see colors or layouts. Use these text signals as proxies for visual aesthetic alignment.

### High Aesthetic Indicators (Score 1 for Aesthetic Marker if 2+ are present)
- Clean, minimal email formatting (plain text or simple markdown, no HTML tables)
- Sender links to a portfolio, Instagram, or website that describes itself using terms like `editorial`, `organic`, `hand-painted`, `quiet`, `breathable`, `soft palette`, `watercolor`, `minimal`
- Signature is understated (name, one link, no banners, no animated GIFs, no tracking pixels)
- Pitch uses calm, unhurried language (`would love to explore`, `no pressure`, `whenever feels right`)

### Low Aesthetic Indicators (Score 0 for Aesthetic Marker if 1+ is present)
- Aggressive HTML formatting (multiple font sizes, colored text, bold spam headers)
- Excessive emojis in business context (🚀💰🔥🚨)
- Signature contains promotional banners, Calendly blocks, or newsletter signup widgets
- Pitch language is hype-driven (`crush it`, `explode your growth`, `viral potential`, `massive ROI`)
- Tracking links or UTM parameters in the pitch body (signals automated outreach tool)

## Prohibited Elements (Automatic Disqualifiers)

If any of the following are detected in the pitch, immediately assign `STATUS: [DEAL-BREAKER ARCHIVE]`. Do not score further.

- **Crypto, forex, trading, NFT, blockchain** (unless explicitly a Christian stewardship resource with no investment pitch)
- **Gambling, betting, sweepstakes**
- **Multi-level marketing (MLM), dropshipping, "passive income" schemes**
- **Adult content, dating apps, relationship coaching with explicit content**
- **New Age / Spiritualism:** Astrology, tarot, manifesting, law of attraction, "energy work," psychic services
- **Aggressive Sales / Growth Hacking:** Funnel building, "scale to 7 figures," viral loop tactics, clickbait
- **Substance-related:** CBD, alcohol-forward brands, vaping (unless explicitly a Christian recovery testimony resource)
